Training

Flashmob Learning Services supports our clients throughout their Flashmob journey by providing educational offerings designed to help you realize the full value of the Flashmob solution.
We offer a range of product subjects and delivery mediums which enable us to customize the learning experience to meet your unique needs and budget.

Types of Training

ON-DEMAND LEARNING

Whether you are a new or experienced user, Flashmob offers comprehensive self-paced courses focused on enhancing utilization of your Flashmob products, administering your Flashmob environment, or both. New courses are added regularly and are available to all clients. Clients can access Flashmob directly through their Flashmob environment.

PUBLIC TRAINING SESSIONS

The Flashmob Learning Services Team offers virtual webinars designed to enhance your knowledge of the Flashmob platform and expose you the latest and greatest capabilities. Topics and the delivery schedule vary from month to month and are advertised via email and/or upon login to your Flashmob environment.

PRIVATE TRAINING CLASSES

We offer custom classes created specifically for your needs and delivered using your Flashmob environment. An instructor will meet with you to scope the engagement and identify the appropriate content and duration. Engagements are available onsite or as
a
series of virtual instructor-led sessions.
